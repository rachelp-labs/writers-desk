Quick Reference

Registration & disclosure at a glance

Key requirements for the EU and US. For detailed decision logic, visit the Tools page.

EU / EEA — CTIS
Registration
SystemCTIS — euclinicaltrials.eu
RegulationEU CTR No 536/2014
MandatoryYes — all phases, no exemptions
In force31 Jan 2022 (new trials); EudraCT closed 30 Jan 2025
Register byBefore trial start — CTA submission
ValidationUp to 10 days
Assessment45–60 days (Part I/II); 30 days if low-intervention
RMSDesignated for multinational trials
RFI response10 days (validation); 12 days (assessment) — no email alerts
Results Posting
CSR deadline30 days from EoT — all categories
Lay summary12 months (6 months if paediatric population)
Versions requiredUnredacted + redacted for publication
Phase exemptionsNone — Cat. 1, 2, and 3 all required
Key Watchouts
IB placementNever in SmPC slot — publishes automatically
PASSInterventional: CTIS + ENCePP; Non-interventional: ENCePP only
EudraCTStill required for PIP/Art. 46 trials outside EEA only
UK sitesAlways require separate MHRA CTA — CTIS does not cover UK
US — ClinicalTrials.gov
Registration
SystemClinicalTrials.gov / PRS — NIH/NLM
RegulationFDAAA 801; 42 CFR Part 11; FDARA 2022
MandatoryYes — ACTs only
ACT definitionPhase II–IV drug/biologic/device with US sites or under IND/IDE
Register byWithin 21 days of first participant enrolment
Phase IGenerally exempt from mandatory registration
NIH-fundedAll phases mandatory regardless of ACT status
Protocol uploadFull protocol required before first enrolment
Recruitment statusMust be kept current throughout trial
Results Posting
Results deadlineWithin 12 months of PCD
PCD defined asDate last participant measured for primary outcome — not last visit or DB lock
AE tableMandatory — submitted alongside results data
Phase I resultsNo mandatory results submission
Delayed reportingGood cause certification available — conditions apply
Key Watchouts
Outcome measuresPre-specified before enrolment as structured data fields
Results formatEntered directly into CT.gov results module — not a document upload
ICMJERegistration required before first enrolment for journal publication
FDARA 2022Extended requirements — check FDA Guidance Jan 2023 for updates
