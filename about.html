About Writers' Desk.

Writers' Desk is a practical reference for medical writers working in clinical trials covering registration and disclosure requirements for the EU (CTIS) and US (ClinicalTrials.gov). This site will be expanded over time to include additional regulatory information and health authority requirements.
Last updated April 2026
