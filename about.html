About

What this site covers

Writers' Desk is a practical reference for medical writers working in clinical trials. It covers registration and disclosure requirements for the EU (CTIS) and US (ClinicalTrials.gov), including results posting obligations, key deadlines, and submission watchouts. The site also provides interactive decision trees, a quick reference guide, a glossary of regulatory terms, and curated updates as requirements evolve.
Last updated April 2026
